The Timeline

The Timeline:

Time Events
1959 Red is born. Red confirmed at the start of Season 6 that he was turning 60, making his birth year 1959.
Circa 1970s Red grows up. Red’s stories from his childhood paint the portrait of someone coming of age with a bit of a rebellious streak.
1972 – 1977 Red works. Before he took over Raymond Reddington’s identity, Red worked jobs throughout his teens. He installed carpet at Lake Charlevoix and did a summer job at the Emmet county fair.
1977 – 1990 A blank space. Red is not Raymond Reddington, so none of Reddington's history applies.
1990(?) Raymond Reddington dies. Red was present at the fire that consumed the house where Raymond Reddington had taken Liz/Masha.
Circa 1990 The imposter strikes. Katarina meets up with her dear friend from childhood, Ilya Koslov.
Christmas 1990 Raymond Reddington disappears. This is when the world believes they have lost all official contact with Raymond Reddington.
1995 The imposter’s crime spree begins. Raymond Reddington got back on the radar due to a classified document leak in 1995, which was traced to him.
1985 Liz/Masha is born. Whoever Red is, he knows intimate details about what Katarina went through when she learned she was pregnant with Masha/Liz.
2003 Red meets Liz. On the same day that Liz starts her new job as an FBI profiler, Raymond Reddington, the FBI’s most wanted man, turns himself in. 
    Written By Britt Lawrence
